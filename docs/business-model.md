# Business Model

Revenue should start early and expand as the ecosystem matures.

## Early revenue

- Paid TypeScript SaaS starter kit
- Founding cohort with live build sessions
- Subscription for access to curriculum, repo, and community

## Mid-stage revenue

- Premium templates and generators
- Team workspaces and collaboration tools
- Boilerplate licensing and white-label packages

## Long-term revenue

- Marketplace transaction fees
- Managed hosting and CI/CD services
- Enterprise onboarding, support, and architecture packages

## Positioning

Learn TypeScript by building real products, collaborating in production workflows, and shipping assets you can use, sell, or grow into a business.
